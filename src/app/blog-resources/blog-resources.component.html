Frequently Asked Questions

How much extra payment should I make?

Even small amounts help. Try $100 extra per month to start. As your income increases, increase the extra payment. Our calculator shows exactly how much you'll save at any amount.

Will paying off my mortgage early hurt my credit score?

No. While paying off debt doesn't boost your credit, it doesn't hurt it either. In fact, having less debt can improve your credit-to-income ratio over time.

Are there penalties for early payoff?

Most mortgages have no prepayment penalty. However, some loans (especially FHA, VA, or USDA loans) may have restrictions. Check your mortgage documents or contact your lender to confirm.

Should I pay off my mortgage or invest the money?

This depends on your mortgage rate, risk tolerance, and investment opportunities. See our blog post "Mortgage Payoff vs. Investing" for a detailed comparison. Many successful people do both.
