Understanding Your Mortgage Payment

Your monthly mortgage payment typically includes principal, interest, property taxes, and homeowners insurance (often referred to as PITI). Our calculator provides a complete breakdown of these costs.

The principal portion goes toward reducing your loan balance, while interest is the cost of borrowing money. Property taxes and insurance are escrowed items that your lender collects monthly.
